Review a workflow with EPT

If a manual process is creating missed work or repeated administration, EPT can help identify the simplest sensible fit: nano CRM, optional Billing, configuration, data migration, or a separately scoped private module.

What to bring to the discussion

  • The business result the workflow must produce
  • Who performs each part of the work
  • Existing spreadsheets, documents, and hand-offs
  • The exceptions that standard software handles poorly
  • What a successful change would look like

EPT does not advertise a general automation suite or unbuilt modules. Any private development is validated, scoped, and quoted separately.
